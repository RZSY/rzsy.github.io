To the uninformed.

How does an "alternate history world" or "alternate universe" work. They work through reimagining what could have happened in historical events, most of them uses a point of divergence. However, DOMINION has multiple points of divergence across different fronts. Some parts, mostly technological developments are made to be nonsensical to a certain point through the usage of exaggeration. This is a work of fiction

Points of Divergence

What are Points of Divergence?

Points of Divergence, refers to events that occurred not as it did in the real world. Points, in this case would refer to multiple events that occurred, and Divergence here is what happened at those points. For example, in the real world, Malaysia existed as a country that is consisted of Tanah Melayu (Malaya), Sabah (North Borneo), and Sarawak. However, in the world of Dominion, the nation known as Malaysia do not exist and is instead known as the Dominion of Malaya and Borneo.

As any other Alternate History project, this one will be using DN-[year] to refer to the "In universe" timeline, and RL-[year] to refer to "the real timeline" whenever it is in comparison. If not specifically mentioned, assume it is DN-[year]. This is to ensure that no misunderstandings occur.

Science Fiction and Fantasy Elements in DOMINION

What are the qualifiers?

DOMINION is a passion project. Combining both historical fiction, fantasy and science fiction together. The historical fiction part is already acknowledge in the above paragraph, however, elements of science fiction (Sci-fi) and fantasia (Fantasy) is also made abundant in the lore.

Sci-fi in DOMINION is generally grounded to an overwhelmingly advanced developments for its era, for example: The Japanese Empire utilises biological weapons created out of prisoners, these weapons can be described as monsters that are extremely violent, meanwhile Nazi Germany utilises mega-weapons that is relative to the Nazi mega-projects of real life world war II and also the undead as slave soldiers. Fantasia elements in DOMINION also exist alongside the already established technological advancements, for example; the fictional formation of the 29th Nachzehrer Division of the Waffen SS - a branch created under the command of Adolf Hitler solely for the development of occult weaponry.

Though the axis forces possess such powers, the allied forces are also subject to this "buff". The Americans fighting force also managed to replicate and combine technology stolen from both the Japanese and Germans, creating autonomous commando machine men for battles deemed as "suicide" or "a mindless charge to meet God". Similarly, the British utilises machines that used both technologies as defences all over the British Isles, protecting them from the Blitz via biological machines that need no sleep. The war in this world was one that ended with the complete destruction of the Axis powers through sheer madness. In this world, most tanks with tracks become obsolete when the entire ground is made out of rotting torn flesh, no infantry uniform is distinguishable in the mud made out of guts, bones and skin. From that experience, the allied forces in this version of the second world war fought almost solely with mechanised infantry when faced against the mindless war machines.

In this world, many parts of Malaya is contaminated with radiation, and is roamed by abominations that survived the purge, these areas were abandoned, the same happened in Borneo, Phillippines and many other places in South East Asia, the Burma Railway was completed and extended all the way to China where Southeast Asian slave labour used by the Japanese numbered in the millions. The railway that now runs from Singapore all the way to Burma, then to China is still maintained by the British, it was expanded even in order to connect the safe pockets of land that is separated by the affliction. For example, in Malaya, the parts that is contaminated is sporadic, with pockets in every state, these places are blockaded and walled off. The inhabitants of those lands are consisted of undesirables, rebels, exiled criminals, and many others who are doomed to roam a dangerous land.
