About Me

Just some backstory, as the saying goes, "Tak kenal maka tak cinta, dah kenal boleh benci"

I am RIZQ SYAHMI BIN MUHAMMAD FUAD, some of my friends call me Rizq, or Reez, or Rizz, or in a certain isolated incident, Rizzler by a man from Ipoh named Fadhli Iman. In my 20 years of life, I have had many, many experiences that some might say blessed and others might describe as terribly unlucky. For example; I have had at least seven (7) near death experiences in different geographical phenotypes and I have once been expelled from school due to a little, teeny, tiny, not-really-that-amazing incident where I got severe depression. As the Anglos of the British Isles would say: "Fun innit, lad?"

Currently, I am a student in this fine educational establishment called UiTM (Universiti Teknologi MARA), located in Kedah, the land of paddy, Nasi Kandaq, and Warung Allahyarham Cik Lan. Fun fact, I somehow became the class representative, and from my experience talking with the other class representatives, I am 100% sure that I am the most weird one out of all of them. If weirdness had a meter, I would probably be weirder than all of the class representatives that have ever existed in the entirety of UiTM and all of it's satellites. So far, in my 1 (one) year and a few months living in this fine state, I have been blessed with a not too glorious tan line around my arms, 20 extra kilograms on my body, and a very strong addiction to rice.

Now, before I tell you lots more about my humble self, I would like to warn everyone that dares to read this. I have a very weird sense of humour that when exposed towards humans that was born post-2010 they would cringe and say stuff along the lines of, "Lawak bapak-bapak" or "dad jokes are not even that funny broski", if that sort of humour is not one that you would partake in, I humbly request that you please bare with me. I can't help it.

I came from the land where most things are possible, a place so magical you could see worms powered by magic transporting people from one place to another (LRT, MRT, Monorel, KTM Komuter), a place where on each corner food is abundant, a place where you can see a river of toxic teh tarik flow every day, and every hour (Sungai Klang), that place is not Narnia (Narnia doesn't exist), it is not Hoth (Snow is a myth here), it is not Kampung Chat, Benta, Pahang (we have buildings that are taller than 4 storeys!), if you guessed KL (Kuala Lipis) you are wrong, but if you guessed KL (Kuala Lumpur), you are absolutely correct! I am from Setiawangsa (KJ5), Kuala Lumpur, Wilayah Persekutuan Kuala Lumpur. Born in Ampang, I have two (2) eyes, two (2) hands, ten (10) fingers, and enough platelets (very rare right!)

I am eccentric beyond help, my favourite shirt is a red floral Hawaiian shirt because I tend to be very chill. I have 23 socks that I have collected over the course of a few years, some have holes in them, some don't. My favourite food is indescribable because only my Mak Tok knows how to make it, she calls it Spaghetti Pedas, it's like a soup of some sort made with beef, prawn, chilli boh (I do not know how to spell it), cheese, tomatoes, and lots of Japanese cabbage. I have many hobbies, and my hobbies would sometimes relate to my projects, which I keep separate from my hobbies because I have a dream that one day I might be able to publish them and get money from them (hopefully) and I can quit working and become a rich dude with 3 cats that are fatter than a loaf of bread but healthy enough that I don't need to bring them to the vet every week because they work as a part-time kidney stone factory.

Though I am not perfect, I also love to cook, my friends say that I am a good cook, I don't know if that is true, but I do know that I like to cook, mostly because I like to eat. I also like to sew, my mum said that it's very feminine once, but I do believe that sewing is not a strictly female thing, if one is to think that, they haven't seen how dysfunctional some families are. I am not going to regail stories about my family because that would explain too much about my personality that people would go from "Rizq is a human" to "Kesian doh si budak Rizq tu, backstory dia macam character comic grimdark". To which I say, don't ask what you don't want to know, I follow that rule very strongly.

You're probably wondering, when am I gonna stop yapping about stuff and all that. To that I say, probably in a few more paragraphs.

I am an avid gamer, so please expect some gaming terminology thrown around.

Other than being absolutely eccentric, I like pretending to be a radio host talking about random stuff. For example, in RPTU last semester, I was so bored that I pretended to be a talk host for KERA.FM, an offshoot of ERA.FM, but more loony. Even without anyone there, I can still yap like my life depended on it.
