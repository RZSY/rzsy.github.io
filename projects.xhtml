Riddex VE v3

At first, the Riddex Video Engine was made solely for IML154's individual assignment, but now, it's a bigger project than the assignment. It started from hopeful optimism of getting this thing done fast, but it revolved into a schizophrenic mental limbo of "I am already this far into this, I can't get out now". This is one of the most toughest projects I have ever had the chance to dabble in, and all of this is just for the sake of putting a video of the Hyper Act consert at Dewan Perdana last year.
